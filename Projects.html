Unity C# Video Game

2D wave-based survival game, inspired by Call of Duty zombies. In each wave, eliminate all enemies to open up the shop. Aim your weapon with the mouse and shoot by clicking, or enter the pause menu (escape key) and toggle auto-fire to have your player automatically shoot wherever you aim without any input. In between each wave, you can either buy a new weapon, upgrade your current weapons, or upgrade your health. Close the shop to start the next wave.
