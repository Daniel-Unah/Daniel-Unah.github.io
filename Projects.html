Unity C# Video Game

Click the Play Game button to open the game up in a full screen window, and click the escape button to end the game. This is Wave Survival, a 2D wave-based survival game, inspired by Call of Duty Zombies I built for a Video Game Programming class at my school using Unity C#. In each wave, eliminate all enemies to open up the shop. Aim your weapon with the mouse and shoot by clicking, or enter the pause menu (escape key) and toggle auto-fire to have your player automatically shoot wherever you aim without any input. In between each wave, you can either buy a new weapon, upgrade your current weapons, or upgrade your health. Close the shop to start the next wave.
